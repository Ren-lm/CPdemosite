This week we will be going on the Path:
with Fawziyah Alebiosu

On the Path is an interview series with CodePath’s student community, highlighting our students’ stories,
accomplishments, and journeys to become software engineers. As they move through college and beyond
many young people doubt the path they’re on. For Fawziyah Alebiosu, there has never been any doubt. “It’s
always been computer science,” she says.
